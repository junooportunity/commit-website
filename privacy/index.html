Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Your Thoughts Are Private

Your decisions are yours. We don't sell your data, read your conversations, or share what you're wrestling with. Your thoughts stay on your device.

Information We Collect

Decision Conversations

Your conversations with Commit AI are stored locally and sent to our AI provider for processing. Conversations are not stored on external servers beyond processing.

Commitment Tracking

Decisions you commit to are stored locally on your device to help you honor your choices.

App Settings

Your preferences and settings are stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide AI-powered decision support
  • Track your commitments so you can honor them
  • Improve app functionality

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification (name, address, phone)
  • Payment information
  • Location data
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking

Third-Party Services

AI Processing

Powers our decision support features. Your conversations are processed but not stored.

Data Storage & Security

All personal data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage. We do not maintain external databases of user information. Your data is protected by your device's security features including encryption and biometric locks.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all app data via Settings > Reset App
  • Clear conversation history at any time
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

Children's Privacy

Commit is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
