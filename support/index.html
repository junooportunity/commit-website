Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. Your conversations and decisions are stored locally on your device. We don't sell your data or share what you're wrestling with.

What decisions can Commit help with?

Anything you're overthinking. Career changes, relationships, where to live, money decisions, or even just what to eat for dinner. Big or small, Commit helps you stop circling and start deciding.

Is Commit a replacement for therapy?

No. Commit is a decision support tool, not a mental health service. If you're dealing with anxiety, depression, or other mental health concerns, please consult a qualified professional.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings > Reset App to clear all your data, or simply uninstall the app. Since all data is stored locally, uninstalling removes everything.
